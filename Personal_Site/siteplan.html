Purpose

The purpose of this website will be to create a professional looking power washing site where users can easily understand what our services are, what we are doing, and how to book an appointment.

Audience

The audience for this website is anyone in Rexburg or surrounding areas who is in need of power washing services.

Dynamic Elements

There will be an array of objects in JS, and a function will alter the DOM to display each one of the services on the webpage.
There will also be a function where users can calculate an esimate based on a square footage they enter onto the webpage.
